Journalen isn't what it used to be. It all happened so fast. Everything went to crap. It's like... everyone's sense of morals just... disappeared. Bad economy made things worse. The jobs started drying up. Then the stores had to shut down. Then a black man was elected President. He was supposed to change things. He didn't.

As more and more people turned to crime and violence the town becomes gripped in fear. Dark times. Journalen needs protection.

There is an animal that lives by night, searches the trashcans and cleans out the garbage. To clean out the trashcan of society I've chosen to become more than a man. I'm the hero Journalen needs. I am... the Coon!

As the world plummets into despair the Coon fights to keep order intact.
